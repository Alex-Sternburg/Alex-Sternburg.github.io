About

Since an early age music has been a large part of my life. Even before I began playing percussion which is my principle instrument at the age of ten, I was a violist. Throughout the years I was fortunate to have the opportunity to audition and perform with many different ensembles. The list includes my high school’s symphonic band, festival Jazz band, marching band, and orchestra, Massachusetts senior districts and state orchestras as well as a member of Berklee College of Music’s Symphonic Wind Ensemble and Percussion Ensemble. To say the least, I understand the process and dedication it takes to contribute to high level groups. Therefore, as I move forward in my studies in Music Business Management with the goal of going to law school, you better believe that I will fight for the rights of all musicians. Please refer to my resume as well as my previous projects which I hope you find useful as you consider me to be a partner in your journey.
